# AI-Driven Book: The Robotic Mind

Welcome to the **AI-Driven Book with Integrated RAG Chatbot**. This educational resource focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence and robotics, with an emphasis on practical applications.

## About This Book

This book is designed for students with basic Python and AI knowledge who are entering the field of humanoid robotics. Each module builds upon the previous one, providing a comprehensive understanding of how AI systems interact with robotic platforms.

## Modules

- **Module 1**: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2) - Learn how ROS 2 serves as middleware for humanoid robot control and embodied intelligence.
  - [Chapter 1: Introduction to ROS 2 and Physical AI](./module-1-ros2/intro-to-ros2-and-physical-ai.md)
  - [Chapter 2: ROS 2 Nodes, Topics, Services, and Actions](./module-1-ros2/ros2-nodes-topics-services-actions.md)
  - [Chapter 3: Bridging Python Agents to ROS 2 with rclpy](./module-1-ros2/bridging-python-agents-ros2-rclpy.md)

  - [Module Summary](./module-1-ros2/summary.md)

## Getting Started

Begin your journey by exploring the modules in the sidebar. Each module contains conceptual explanations, practical examples, and exercises to reinforce your learning.
